# Third-party notices and data scope

- Map rendering: MapLibre GL JS, loaded from jsDelivr. MapLibre is open-source software under its published license.
- Legacy detail mini-maps in the original UI: Leaflet.
- Satellite imagery: MapTiler Satellite when a MapTiler key is configured; Esri World Imagery is the no-key fallback. Provider attribution remains visible on the map.
- Routing test endpoint: OSRM-compatible endpoint configured through `VITE_ROUTING_BASE_URL`.
- Geographic names and operational boundary: derived from the supplied NAV KURD source package, whose dataset metadata references OpenStreetMap/Geofabrik, GeoNames and Iraq COD-AB sources.

The included Kurdistan boundary is an operational application/data-coverage boundary. It is not a legal, political, cadastral, governmental, or surveying certification.
